About Me

This is when I decided to pursue marketing with a data-driven approach, and although I didn't end up in the advertising agency I wanted, I managed to make the most of my chances in the organization I interned in by applying data analysis for marketing projects. (TLDR: I got bored doing just creative stuff in Marketing, so I added in critical thinking and numbers to spice things up).
